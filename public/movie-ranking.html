My Top 3 movies of all time

The Night Agent

Shawn Ryan produced the American action thriller TV series The Night Agent, which is based on Matthew Quirk's novel of the same name. It aired on Netflix on March 23, 2023, and stars Gabriel Basso in the title character. The narrative is that FBI agent Peter Sutherland is drawn into a massive conspiracy involving a mole at the top levels of the US government. To preserve the nation, he embarks on a desperate search for the traitor, while sheltering former tech CEO Rose Larkin from the individuals who murdered her aunt and uncle.
